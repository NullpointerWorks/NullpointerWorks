Introduction

For the majority of people who own a computer treat it as nothing more than a fancy box. A tool that breaks often and is always a hassle to update or kept at reasonable speed. They use it to check their e-mails, view and print documents, update their socials and perhaps play a game of solitair. However, in the eyes of an enthusiast it's not just a fancy box, nor an over-powered calculator. It's a piece of furnature that requires the same love and care like anything else in the household.

Even among plenty of gamers the ability to do maintenance to their PC is considered an important skill. They carefully reasearch and select each component that will eventually turn into a well lubricated machine.
